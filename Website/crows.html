The Crow Collection

In this collection, you will find pictures of my murder of crows. In 2023, I discovered how smart they can be; in fact, crows can remember the faces of both the people they love and hate. When they find someone who feeds them, they begin leaving you presents, following you around their territory (to make sure you're safe), and will often become protective of you.
